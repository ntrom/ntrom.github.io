Grateful Stratego

Grateful Stratego is a Grateful-Dead themed version of the classic board game Stratego.

Multiple player accounts supported
Choose from different options to play and replay your games

Play against an easy or hard computer player - or watch two of them face each other.
Set up your board - drag and drop pieces to custom locations, randomize locations, or choose from existing setups

Now that you're all set up it's time to start the game!
Once it's your turn, pick a piece to move and drag and drop it to an adjacent location

Capture other pieces and tally them up
You can always save your game and resume playing another time

All game devs need cheats to make our lives easier!
View records, and view replays of previous games.

Enjoy the game!
